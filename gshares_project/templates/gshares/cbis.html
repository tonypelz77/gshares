BUSINESS SUMMARY

Cannabis Science, Inc. develops, produces, and commercializes phytocannabinoid-based pharmaceutical products. It develops medicines to treat autism, blood pressure, cancer and cancer side effects, and other illnesses, including general health maintenance. The company is developing CS-TATI-1 for newly diagnosed and treatment-experienced patients with drug-resistant HIV strains; CS-S/BCC-1 for basal and squamous cell carcinomas; and a proprietary cannabis-based therapy for neurological conditions. In addition, it provides an online video-based medical cannabis education system comprising various courses, such as medical cannabis law, medical marijuana, cooking, horticulture, and bud tending. The company has a license agreement with Apothecary Genetics Investments LLC. to produce various brand formulations for California medical cannabis market. Cannabis Science, Inc. is based in Colorado Springs, Colorado.
