GanjaShares>

Founded and based in the Mile High City of Denver, Colorado, GanjaShares is at the epicenter of the emerging and high growth marijuana industry. GanjaShares was founded to be a source of information, news and analytics on publicly-listed companies active in the marijuana industry - please note GanjaShares is not an investment advisor nor do we provide any investment advice, read our Disclaimer for more information.

The GanjaShares site (currently) features the following:

  • Stocks: GanjaShares maintains a database of quick access profiles, charts, comments and links to all publicly-listed companies active in the marijuana industry. If you don't see a company, tell us and we will do our best to include it as fast as possible. We are constantly looking for ways to improve our company profiles in terms of information, format, etc. - please contact us if you have any cool ideas or requests.

  • Forums: GanjaShares is actively building a community for those interested in discussing this high growth sector. We intend to keep our Forums free and open to the public - register and start interacting today.

  • News & Articles: Our site includes links to popular articles from the various marijuana industry publications we follow - we add new links to resources frequently so check back often. GanjaShares also (of course) maintains a stock-specific news database which is constantly updated. Once (if) our lawyers ok it, we intend to publish reader submitted articles - stay tuned.

  • Tools: The site has several tools for readers including the GanjaShares twitter feed and a (stock) charting app. Yes, we know Forums is also a "tool" but we thought it would get more attention on the primary navbar.

Feel free to contact us with questions/suggestions about the site or any other issue(s). For advertising inquires please contact our sales team.
