BUSINESS SUMMARY

MyECheck, Inc. operates in the payment processing industry. The company provides electronic check services to businesses, payment services providers, financial institutions, and other government entities. It offers electronic check service that enable companies to accept check payments from consumers and businesses online or over a mobile device; and check authorization service, which enables merchants to verify consumer provided data, check the status of the customers bank account, provide evidence that the consumer has authorized the check, and predict the check being returned unpaid. The company also provides check guarantee services that warranty all approved checks and reimburse the payee for financial losses incurred as a result of returned checks; and G-Pay service, an electronic payment service for government. MyECheck, Inc. was founded in 2004 and is headquartered in Folsom, California.
