BUSINESS SUMMARY

Terra Tech Corporation is engaged in the design, marketing, and sale of hydroponic equipment in the United States. The company integrates hydroponic equipment with proprietary technology to create sustainable solutions for the cultivation of indoor agriculture. It offers portable hydroponic trailers, and The Big Bud and Little Bud, which are custom fabricated proprietary cultivation systems; magnetic ballasts, cultivation tents, digital atmospheric controllers, and recycling timers, as well as a line of nutrients; and hydroponic produce. The company also provides its products through its terratechcorp.com Website. Its products are primarily used by companies, horticulture enthusiasts, local urban farmers, and green house growers. Terra Tech Corporation was founded in 2010 and is headquartered in Irvine, California.
